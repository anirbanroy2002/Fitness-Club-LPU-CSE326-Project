About Us

Our Goal

Ever since the dawn of time, humans have been fascinated by the idea of being fit and healthy. However, in this day and age, the busy lifestyle of us humans has made it difficult for us to maintain a healthy lifestyle. We at FitNFine, aim to make it easier for you to maintain a healthy lifestyle by providing you with the best fitness and nutrition services.



Our Journey

Learn about our journey, how we became the #1 fitness club in the state of Punjab.


2015

The Beginning

We started our journey in 2015 with a single gym in the city of Jalandhar. We had a vision to provide the best fitness services to the people of Punjab.

2017

Expansion

We expanded our services to the city of Ludhiana in 2017. We opened our second gym in the city of Ludhiana.

2019

The Big Leap

In 2019, we expanded our services to the city of Amritsar. We opened our third gym in the city of Amritsar.

2021

The Future

In 2021, we expanded our services to the city of Patiala. We opened our fourth gym in the city of Patiala.
